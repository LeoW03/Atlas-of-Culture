# The Democracy Satisfaction Paradox
**Slug:** democracy-satisfaction-paradox | **Status:** AWAITING SIGN-OFF

## The angle
Switzerland has 45% voter turnout — lowest in Western Europe — and 83% democratic satisfaction. The US has 55% turnout and 30% satisfaction. Belgium has compulsory voting (89% turnout) and 50% satisfaction. Voting more doesn't make you feel more heard. Switzerland's secret: direct democracy via referendum means every citizen can actually *change things* between elections.
