# Free Press, Mistrusted Press
**Slug:** free-press-trust | **Status:** AWAITING SIGN-OFF

## The angle
The US ranks 55th in press freedom — and only 32% of Americans trust the media. Finland ranks 1st and 69% of Finns trust the media. But the surprise isn't the Finland comparison. It's that the US has *more* freedom than 130+ countries, and *less* trust than most of them. Freedom and trust are different variables.
