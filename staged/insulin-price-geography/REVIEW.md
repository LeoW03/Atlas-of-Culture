# The Insulin Price Gap
**Slug:** insulin-price-geography | **Status:** AWAITING SIGN-OFF

## The angle
The US pays 8× more than comparable wealthy nations for the same insulin — not 8× more than Pakistan. 8× more than Germany. That peer comparison is the surprise. One country, alone on the map.
